Our Objective

Bringing transparency and accountability to the monitoring of school infrastructure to ensure quality education.

What is Our Goal?

Our primary goal is to create a citizen-centric platform where anyone can directly report the status of school infrastructure. By utilizing AI and IoT, we ensure rapid identification, verification, and resolution of issues, minimizing the time taken for manual inspection.

Why is This Important?

  • Quick Resolution: Complaints are prioritized for immediate action.
  • Accountability: GPS-based verification ensures officers can only confirm action when physically present on-site.
  • Citizen Engagement: Empowering citizens to directly participate in governance and witness improvements.

How the System Works

1. Citizen Report

Citizens take a picture of the school's condition using a live webcam and file a complaint.

2. Prioritization & Monitoring

Complaints are prioritized based on categories like 'Worst', 'Bad', and 'Good'.

3. Official Verification

Officers visit the site and upload a GPS-verified photo of the action taken within 100 meters of the location.
